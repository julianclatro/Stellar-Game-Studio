## Suspect Profiles

### 1. Victor Ashford — Business Partner (Study)

- **Alibi:** Claims he was reviewing documents in the study all evening
- **Dialogue:**
  - *Default:* "I was reviewing documents all evening. This is a tragedy."
  - *Insurance docs:* "Those documents are routine business. Every partner has them."
  - *Crumpled note:* "...Where did you find that? It's not what it looks like."
  - *Confrontation (perfume_bottle + insurance_docs):* "You don't understand the pressure I was under. He was going to ruin everything."
- **Truth:** THE KILLER. Poisoned the victim for the insurance payout.

### 2. Elena Castillo — Personal Chef (Kitchen)

- **Alibi:** Says she was cooking and never left the kitchen
- **Dialogue:**
  - *Default:* Claims she was preparing the dessert course
  - *Missing knife:* Insists she had a full set — becomes agitated
- **Truth:** Innocent. The missing knife is a red herring.

### 3. Dr. Marcus Webb — Family Doctor (Lounge)

- **Alibi:** Claims he was on an hour-long phone call in the lounge
- **Dialogue:**
  - *Default:* Discusses the victim's health concerns
  - *Phone records:* Alibi collapses — only a 2-minute call
  - *Confrontation:* Admits he saw Victor leaving the bedroom corridor
- **Truth:** Innocent but covering for his short absence. Key witness.

### 4. Isabelle Fontaine — Art Dealer & Ex-Lover (Bedroom)

- **Alibi:** Found near the body, says she discovered it
- **Dialogue:**
  - *Default:* Distraught, describes finding the body
  - *Perfume bottle:* Recognizes it as NOT the victim's scent
- **Truth:** Innocent. Genuinely discovered the body. Red herring suspect.

### 5. Thomas Grey — Groundskeeper (Garden)

- **Alibi:** Says he was tending the garden and saw nothing
- **Dialogue:**
  - *Default:* Talks about the garden work
  - *Camera photos:* Garden was empty at 8 PM — alibi broken
- **Truth:** Innocent. Was sneaking a cigarette break, not murdering anyone. Muddy footprints are his.

### 6. Priya Sharma — Accountant (Kitchen)

- **Alibi:** Claims she was helping Elena in the kitchen
- **Dialogue:**
  - *Default:* Corroborates Elena's story
- **Truth:** Innocent. Provides partial alibi for Elena.

### 7. James Whitmore — Lawyer (Study)

- **Alibi:** Says he was with Victor reviewing contracts
- **Dialogue:**
  - *Default:* Confirms he was in the study with Victor
  - *Insurance docs:* Admits Victor was nervous all evening and stepped out for "fresh air"
- **Truth:** Innocent. Key witness — reveals Victor's suspicious behavior.

### 8. Celeste Duval — Victim's Sister (Lounge)

- **Alibi:** Claims she was chatting with Dr. Webb
- **Dialogue:**
  - *Default:* Expresses grief, discusses inheritance
- **Truth:** Innocent. Has motive (inheritance) but a solid alibi with Dr. Webb.

### 9. Ren Nakamura — Photographer/Guest (Garden)

- **Alibi:** Says he was photographing the garden at dusk
- **Dialogue:**
  - *Default:* Shows enthusiasm about the garden photos
  - *Camera photos:* His timestamps contradict Thomas's alibi
- **Truth:** Innocent. His photos are key evidence against Thomas's alibi.
